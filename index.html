FAQ

Questions school leaders usually ask first.

Is this encouraging cheating?

No. The program focuses on responsible use, disclosure, academic integrity, and assessment redesign.

Do students need personal AI accounts?

No. Sessions can be teacher-guided and age-appropriate, based on your school's comfort level.

Is this coding-focused?

No. Coding can be included later, but the foundation is AI literacy, ethics, critical thinking, and safe usage.

Can parents be included?

Yes. Parent communication is important because trust improves when families understand boundaries and safeguards.

Can this align with our board and school culture?

Yes. We adapt the policy, training, and student sessions to your institution's age groups and academic expectations.

What happens after the first session?

You can choose a paid pilot, faculty certification program, student classes, policy support, or a school-wide rollout.
